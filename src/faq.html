Payment Options

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • GCash - 0917 528 3832
  • Cash - Upon check-in

Is down payment required?

Yes, we require 50% down payment to confirm your reservation. The remaining balance can be settled upon check-in.

When is full payment required?

Full payment is required upon check-in. For large groups or events, we may require full payment 7 days before arrival.

Cancellation Policy

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • 7+ days before check-in - Full refund
  • 3-6 days before check-in - 50% refund
  • 48 hours or less - No refund
  • No show - No refund

What about weather cancellations?

In case of typhoon signals or extreme weather conditions where travel is dangerous, we offer:

  • Full refund or
  • Rescheduling to available dates within 3 months
